My Internships

United Airlines

During the 2023 summer, I was a Powerplant Engineering Intern at United Airlines in Chicago, IL. During my time, I was responsible for the maintenance, overhaul, and research of the V2500 engines used on the Airbus 319/320. When engines would decommission, it was my job to analyze and route their maintenance, which included on-site engine visits nationally and globally. I also conducted research into various faulty components of V2500 using Excel and Palantir to research how and when damages occurred and develop mitigating actions to reduce maintenance costs.

Georgia Tech Research Institute

During the 2022 summer, I was a Mechanical Research Intern at Georgia Tech Research Institute in Tucson, AZ. During my time, I was responsible for designing various mechanical projects for aircraft usage. My main project was a storage system for the Air Natural Guard to store ECM pods. I used SolidWorks for its design and FEA software to analyze how it would handle a load of 10 tons. I also worked on QRIP assemblies for installation in F-16 and A-10 cockpits and on a SolidWorks & Excel hardware fasteners library for the Air Force Reserve base. My experience with SolidWorks during this internship helped me pass the Certified SolidWorks Professional exam.

Boson Motors

Before my internship at Georgia Tech, I worked at Boson Motors in Livermore, CA, as a Testing & Validation Intern. During this time, I assisted with code reviews and performed QA testing for an electric truck's autonomous driving using Python. I also did work with a technician and helped integrate mechanical and data collection instruments onto the truck.
